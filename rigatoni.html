Sausage Rigatoni with Cream Sauce

Description

Large pasta tubes with chunks of delicious, spicy sausage, all slathered in a thick, cream-based sauce and topped with parmesan cheese!

Ingredients

  • Rigatoni pasta
  • Italian sausage
  • Tomato paste
  • Onion, diced
  • Red peppers, diced
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parsley
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Begin boiling water for the pasta.
  2. Brown sausage at the bottom of a large pot.
  3. Add onions and red peppers.
  4. When sausage is cooked through, add tomato paste.
  5. Add pasta to boiling water.
  6. Stir heavy cream into the sausage mixture.
  7. Once rigatoni is cooked, drain and stir into sausage and sauce.
  8. Serve with mozzarella, parmesan and parsley!
